Balance of research and development

THDC is the national agricultural research center with “development” in its mandate. Many farmers today grow vegetable and fruits using seed or technologies developed/provided by the Center.

Growing with THDC

Farmers are learning improved vegetable and fruit production practices from trainings provided by THDC, a project to encourage young people to take up horticulture as a career.

For prosperity and health

THDC conducts research, builds networks, and carries out training and promotion activities to raise awareness of the role of fruits and vegetables for improved health.
